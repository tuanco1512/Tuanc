Shipping & Returns

Please allow 10-14 days additional ship time for all orders containing monogrammed items. All monogrammed items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.

Engraved items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged. Master & Dynamic's standard warranty policy still applies to custom engraved orders.

We will email you the tracking information for your shipment once your order leaves our warehouse.

Replacement items ship complimentary UPS Ground, or equivalent services in other regions.
